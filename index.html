Features and Updates

 

 Welcome to the Future of the RiverSMP World.

  • RiverSMP is a future-themed survival server with a goal of being a community-based hangout for people to communicate and explore.

  • Grinding, Questing, Hunting, and Mining is essential for your path to leveling up and achieving new heights on the server.

  • We have a variety of features such as McMMO, Shops, an Auction House, Claiming System, and More!

  • Our community is a friendly group as you will be able to meet new people and join a team as you adventure the vast world of RiverSMP.

  • Chestshops

  • And a whole lot more!
